IMAGINARY AUTHOR X PENGUIN RANDOM HOUSE

Imaginary Authors seeks to ignite memories for authors, readers, and writers alike. These memories, while diverse in origin, are rooted in the brand's initial inspiration: books. In celebration of this connection, Imaginary Authors has partnered with Penguin Random House to offer 7 bestselling eBooks paired with the top 7 bestselling scents from IA.

This collaboration allows readers to discover their perfect scent inspired by their favorite book, bridging the gap between storytelling and fragrance. But the excitement doesn’t stop there—this initiative also serves as a creative contest. Readers are invited to submit their own original stories inspired by these evocative scents for a chance to be featured.

The 7 iconic scents featured in this collaboration are:

Cape Heartache

Fox in the Flowerbed

Memoirs of a Trespasser

Saint Julep

Slow Explosions

In Love with Everything

A Whiff of Waffle Cone
